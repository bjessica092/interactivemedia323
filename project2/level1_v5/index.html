"Hope is the thing



with feathers -

That perches




in the soul -

And sings the tune



without the words -

And never




stops - at all -

And sweetest - in the Gale -



is heard -

And sore


must be the storm -

That could abash the




little Bird

That kept so





many warm -

I've heard it in the



chillest land -

And on the




strangest Sea -

Yet - never - in





Extremity,

It asked a


crumb - of me.
